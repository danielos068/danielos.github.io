Car Repairs and Services

Routine Maintenance

  • Oil Change: Regularly replacing engine oil and oil filter.
  • Tire Rotation: Rotating tires to ensure even wear.
  • Brake Inspection: Checking brake pads, rotors, and fluid levels.
  • Battery Check: Testing and maintaining the car battery.
  • Fluid Checks: Inspecting and topping off various fluids (coolant, transmission fluid, etc.).

Common Repairs

  • Tire Replacement: Replacing worn or damaged tires.
  • Brake Repair: Replacing brake pads, rotors, and calipers.
  • Battery Replacement: Installing a new car battery.
  • Exhaust System Repair: Fixing or replacing exhaust components.
  • Ignition System Repair: Replacing spark plugs and ignition coils.

Electrical Repairs

  • Headlight Replacement: Changing burnt-out headlights.
  • Wiring Issues: Fixing electrical wiring problems.
  • Alternator Repair: Replacing or repairing the alternator.
  • Fuse Replacement: Replacing blown fuses.
  • Electrical System Diagnostics: Identifying and fixing electrical faults.

Cooling System Repairs

  • Radiator Flush: Flushing and refilling the radiator.
  • Coolant Replacement: Changing the coolant.
  • Thermostat Replacement: Installing a new thermostat.
  • Water Pump Repair: Fixing or replacing the water pump.
  • Heater Core Repair: Replacing or repairing the heater core.

Transmission Repairs

  • Transmission Fluid Change: Replacing transmission fluid.
  • Transmission Rebuild: Rebuilding or replacing the transmission.
  • Clutch Replacement: Replacing the clutch in manual transmissions.
  • Torque Converter Repair: Fixing or replacing the torque converter.
  • Transmission Diagnostics: Identifying and fixing transmission issues.

Air Conditioning Repairs

  • A/C Recharge: Recharging the air conditioning system.
  • Compressor Replacement: Installing a new A/C compressor.
  • Condenser Repair: Fixing or replacing the condenser.
  • Evaporator Repair: Replacing or repairing the evaporator.
  • A/C Diagnostics: Identifying and fixing A/C issues.

Suspension and Steering Repairs

  • Shock Absorber Replacement: Installing new shock absorbers.
  • Strut Replacement: Replacing struts for better ride quality.
  • Tie Rod Replacement: Fixing or replacing tie rods.
  • Wheel Alignment: Adjusting the alignment of the wheels.
  • Steering Rack Repair: Fixing or replacing the steering rack.

Body and Exterior Repairs

  • Paint Touch-Up: Fixing minor paint scratches and chips.
  • Dent Repair: Removing dents from the car body.
  • Windshield Replacement: Replacing a cracked or broken windshield.
  • Bumper Repair: Fixing or replacing damaged bumpers.
  • Glass Repair: Repairing side and rear windows.

Advanced Repairs

  • Engine Overhaul: Rebuilding the engine.
  • Transmission Rebuild: Rebuilding the transmission.
  • Engine Rebuild: Rebuilding the engine for major repairs.
  • Turbocharger Repair: Fixing or replacing the turbocharger.
  • Engine Diagnostics: Identifying and fixing complex engine issues.

High Horsepower Modifications

  • Turbocharger Installation: Adding a turbocharger for increased power.
  • Supercharger Installation: Installing a supercharger to boost engine performance.
  • Performance Exhaust: Upgrading to high-flow exhaust systems for better engine breathing.
  • Engine Tuning: Optimizing engine parameters for maximum performance.
  • Cold Air Intake Systems: Installing cold air intakes to improve air flow and engine efficiency.
